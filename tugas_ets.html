Our Story

Barenbliss, a professional clean beauty brand born out of Seoul, Korea. The Co-Founder of barenbliss, Jina Kim holds the belief that everyone has the right to enjoy "Joyful Clean Beauty" which is vegan, cruelty-free, and non-toxic which results in our "B+N+B" clean philosophy.

  • Bare Essentials
  • Nature Inspired
  • Bliss Moments

We are committed to the Clean Beauty philosophy and have created a list of 2000 banned risk & cruelty-free ingredients at the formulation level. And our products are always dermatologically tested to evaluate the effectiveness and safety of all products that come into contact with the skin's surface.
